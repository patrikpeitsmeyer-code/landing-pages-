# Clay.com Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

## Primary ICP: Growth-Stage B2B SaaS Companies

### Firmographics
- **Company size:** 50-500 employees
- **ARR:** $5M - $100M
- **Stage:** Series A through Series C
- **Industry:** B2B SaaS, Tech, Professional Services
- **GTM model:** Sales-led or hybrid PLG + sales

### Team Structure Signals
- Has dedicated SDR/BDR team (3+ reps)
- Has RevOps or GTM Ops function
- Running outbound as a growth channel
- Using a CRM (HubSpot, Salesforce, or similar)

### Technology Signals (Good Fit)
- **Uses outbound tools:** Outreach, Salesloft, Apollo, Instantly
- **Uses CRM:** Salesforce, HubSpot
- **Has marketing automation:** Marketo, Pardot, HubSpot
- **Invests in data:** Previously used ZoomInfo, Clearbit, or Apollo

### Behavioral Signals
- Recently raised funding (ready to scale GTM)
- Hiring for SDR, RevOps, or GTM Engineer roles
- Leadership posting about outbound/pipeline challenges on LinkedIn
- Attending GTM-focused events or communities

---

## Secondary ICP: Outbound Agencies & Consultants

### Firmographics
- **Company size:** 5-50 employees
- **Revenue:** $500K - $10M
- **Type:** Lead gen agency, outbound agency, GTM consultancy
- **Clients:** Serving multiple B2B companies

### Why They Buy
- Need to run campaigns for multiple clients efficiently
- Can't afford separate tool subscriptions per client
- Need waterfall enrichment to maximize coverage
- Want to deliver better personalization than competitors

---

## Anti-ICP (Who We DON'T Sell To)

### Bad Fit Signals
- **No outbound motion** — Pure inbound or product-led with no sales team
- **Too small** — Pre-seed startups with no dedicated sales/ops
- **Too large & slow** — Enterprise (5000+ employees) with 18-month procurement cycles
- **Wrong industry** — B2C, local businesses, non-tech
- **DIY engineers** — Companies that want to build everything in-house

### Disqualifiers
- No budget for GTM tooling
- No CRM in place
- Founder doing all sales (too early)
- Already deep in competitor ecosystem (locked into ZoomInfo contract)

---

## Key Decision Makers & Influencers

### Primary Buyer
- **VP/Head of Sales** — Owns pipeline targets
- **VP/Head of Revenue Operations** — Owns GTM stack
- **Head of Growth** — Owns experimental GTM motions

### Influencers
- **SDR Manager** — Uses tool daily, feels the pain
- **GTM Engineer** — Technical champion, builds workflows
- **Marketing Ops** — Cares about data quality and integrations

### Economic Buyer
- **CRO/VP Sales** — Approves budget
- **CFO** — Signs off on larger deals

---

## Pain Points That Trigger Purchase

1. "Our enrichment coverage is only 40-60%"
2. "We're paying for 5 different data tools and still have gaps"
3. "Our SDRs spend half their time researching instead of selling"
4. "We can't personalize at scale without hiring more people"
5. "Our CRM data is stale and nobody trusts it"
6. "We just raised funding and need to scale outbound fast"
7. "Our agency clients expect better data than we can deliver"
